About Me

I am a network software engineer/researcher at BYD Company. I received my Ph.D. degree at Shanghai Advanced Research Institute (SARI), Chinese Academy of Sciences. I was also a joint training PhD student at the Graz University of Technology (TU Graz) during November 2021 to October 2023. I received my BSc degree from Donghua University, Shanghai, China in 2018.

My main research interests are in the domain of cyber-physical systems: embedded systems, low power wide area networks and testbeds.
